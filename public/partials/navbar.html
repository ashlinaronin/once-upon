what? how?

Once.upon is an eternal communal fable in the browser; an Elysium of incorporeal narrators; a glocal commons borne of the yarn. Each visiting teller in turn records hirself speaking a sentence. As it is spoken, the contribution is transcribed and saved, then the baton is passed to the next in queue. Visitors may listen back from any point.

Your browser doesn't support recording. If you'd like to add to the story, please use Google Chrome.

A project by Ashlin Aronin and Andre Brown.

This story is being created by many people.

  2. Anyone can listen to the story at one time, but there can only be one active contributor. When your status is 'ready' and the record button is pulsating, you are record enabled. When you're ready, press the record button and start speaking into the headset. You will see your speech transcribed automatically in the record panel, and anyone else logged on to the site will be able to watch your contribution in real time.
  3. Once.upon will stop recording automatically after you finish speaking. If you pause during your contribution, the system may cut you off. This is to encourage quick turnover so many people can participate.
  4. Enjoy!
