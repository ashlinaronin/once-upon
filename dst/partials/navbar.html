Once.upon is an eternal communal fable in the browser; an Elysium of incorporeal narrators; a glocal commons borne of the yarn. Each visiting teller in turn records hirself speaking a sentence. As it is spoken, the contribution is transcribed and saved, then the baton is passed to the next in queue. Visitors may listen back from any point.

Your browser doesn't support recording. If you'd like to add to the story, please use Google Chrome.

A project by Ashlin Aronin and Andre Brown.
